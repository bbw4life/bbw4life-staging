SlitLux Dress — Size Guide

The SlitLux Dress uses plus size XL labeling. Measure your bust, waist and hips. If between sizes, go up — especially with fitted long-sleeve styles featuring cutout details.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.
