PaisleyBelt Dress — Size Guide

Designed with a relaxed waist-belted fit that flatters curvy silhouettes. The belt adjusts for your ideal waist definition. When between sizes, size up for comfortable movement.

How to Measure Your Body

  • Bust: Around the fullest part of your chest, tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Around the narrowest part of your natural waist.
  • Hips: Around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.
