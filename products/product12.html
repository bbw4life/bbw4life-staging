PaisleyBelt Dress — Size Guide

The PaisleyBelt Dress is designed with a relaxed waist-belted fit that flatters and celebrates curvy silhouettes. The included belt can be adjusted for your ideal waist definition. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfortable, flowing movement.

How to Measure Your Body

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size for maximum comfort and confidence.

How do I care for this dress?
›

Washing: Hand wash in cold water with gentle detergent. If using a machine, use a delicate cycle in a mesh laundry bag. Avoid hot water to preserve the richness of the orange paisley print.

Drying: Lay flat to dry or hang gently. Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods to maintain the vibrancy of the orange tones.

Ironing: Use low heat with a pressing cloth between the iron and the fabric. Iron inside out to protect the print. Queens who care for their PaisleyBelt Dress report wearing it 25+ times with no color loss or shape distortion.
