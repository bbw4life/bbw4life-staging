BlushLace Gown — Plus Size Size Guide

The BlushLace Gown has an empire waist construction — it gathers just below the bust and flows freely from there. The bust measurement is the most critical fit point. If between sizes, choose your usual size — the chiffon skirt accommodates generously.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest — primary measurement for empire waist gowns.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • Between sizes? Choose your usual size. Chiffon drapes generously.

Is an empire waist gown flattering for a plus size body?
+

The empire waist is one of the most universally flattering silhouettes for plus size bodies. By gathering just below the bust and releasing freely from there, it creates an elongated A-line shape that flows beautifully over the hips and stomach — no defined waist required. The empire construction in the BlushLace Gown was positioned specifically for plus size bodies to maximize the flattering effect from 1XL through 5XL.

Does blush pink work for darker skin tones?
+

Blush pink is particularly striking on deeper, richer skin tones — it creates a warm, luminous contrast that reads as romantic and radiant. On lighter skin tones it carries an ethereal quality. Every queen looks breathtaking in it. This is by design — the colour was chosen because it flatters universally across all skin tones and all backgrounds.
