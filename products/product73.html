Is an empire waist gown flattering for plus size bodies?
+

The empire waist is one of the most flattering silhouettes for plus size bodies. It gathers just below the bust and flows freely from there — creating an elongated A-line that drapes beautifully over hips and curves from 1XL to 5XL.

Does blush pink work for darker skin tones?
+

Blush pink is particularly striking on deeper skin tones — warm, luminous and radiant. On lighter tones it reads as ethereal. This colour was chosen because every queen looks breathtaking in it.
