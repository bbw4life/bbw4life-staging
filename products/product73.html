BlushLace Gown — Plus Size Size Guide

The BlushLace Gown has an empire waist construction — it gathers just below the bust and flows freely from there. This means the bust measurement is the most critical fit point. If between sizes, choose your usual size — the chiffon skirt accommodates generously. For a more relaxed feel through the bodice, size up one.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest — this is the primary measurement for empire waist gowns.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • Empire construction flows freely below the bust — hips are less critical to fit.
  • Between sizes? Choose your usual size. Chiffon drapes generously.

Is an empire waist gown flattering for a plus size body?
+

The empire waist is one of the most universally flattering silhouettes for plus size bodies. By gathering just below the bust — the narrowest point of the torso — and releasing freely from there, the empire cut creates an elongated A-line shape that flows beautifully over the hips and stomach. It does not require a defined waist measurement to look elegant. The flowing chiffon skirt of the BlushLace Gown cascades from the empire seam in a way that creates movement and grace with every step.

At BBW4LIFE, this gown was not modified from a standard pattern. The empire construction was positioned specifically for plus size bodies — higher and closer to the natural underbust line — to maximize the flattering effect for queens 1XL through 5XL.
