The PlaidOverall Dress uses plus size XL labeling. Measure your bust, waist and hips. If between sizes, go up — especially important with dungaree-style wide-strap dresses to ensure comfortable shoulder coverage.

How to Measure

  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
