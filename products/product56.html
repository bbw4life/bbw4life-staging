How do I remove waterproof mascara without damaging my lashes?
›

Always use an oil-based makeup remover or micellar water designed for waterproof formulas. Soak a cotton pad, press it against your closed eye for 15–20 seconds to dissolve the formula, then gently swipe downward. Never rub or scrub. Rubbing weakens lashes over time. With the right remover, this mascara comes off cleanly and completely without pulling a single lash.

Can I use this mascara for a natural daytime look or is it only for dramatic looks?
›

One coat gives a natural, enhanced look. Two coats give full drama. The formula and wand give you complete control over the intensity. For a natural daytime look, apply one coat with a light hand — focusing on the outer lashes to open the eyes. For evening or events, build it up to two or three coats for the full volumizing effect. Same mascara. Different queens. Different moments.

Does it work on short or straight lashes?
›

Especially on short or straight lashes. The 4D curling wand was designed to lift lashes that naturally resist curl — it physically wraps and holds the curl as the formula sets. Straight lashes are gripped from the root and lifted upward, creating a visible opening of the eye that shorter lashes often struggle to achieve with standard formulas. Curl first with a lash curler, then apply the mascara while lashes are still warm — the results are dramatic.
