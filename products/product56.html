How do I remove waterproof mascara without damaging my lashes?
›

Use an oil-based remover or micellar water for waterproof formulas. Soak a cotton pad, press against your closed eye for 15–20 seconds, then gently swipe down. Never rub — it comes off cleanly with the right remover.

Can I use this for a natural look or only for drama?
›

One coat = natural. Two to three coats = full drama. Same mascara, different queens, different moments.

Does it work on short or straight lashes?
›

Especially on short or straight lashes. The 4D wand grips from the root and lifts upward. Curl with a lash curler first, apply while lashes are still warm — results are dramatic.
