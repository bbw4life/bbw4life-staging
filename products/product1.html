Glam Heels — Size Guide

These heels are designed to celebrate every curve. Measure your foot length from heel to toe and match below. When between sizes, go up for maximum comfort.

How to Measure Your Foot

  • Step 1: Place your foot flat on a piece of paper.
  • Step 2: Mark the tip of your longest toe and the back of your heel.
  • Step 3: Measure the distance between the two marks in cm.
  • When between sizes, choose the larger size — comfort first, always.
