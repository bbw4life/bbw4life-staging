Smart Hula Hoop — Size Guide & Assembly

The hoop is fully modular — add or remove sections to match your waist. Measure around your natural waist (just above the navel). Sizes in inches (in) and centimeters (cm).

How to Measure Your Waist

  • Step 1: Stand upright, breathe out naturally.
  • Step 2: Wrap the tape around your natural waistline — just above your navel.
  • Step 3: Keep the tape flat and parallel to the floor.
  • When between two sizes, choose the larger size for comfort.

Assembly (2 minutes, no tools)

  • Step 1: Lay all sections on a flat surface.
  • Step 2: Snap sections together — each piece clicks into the next.
  • Step 3: Connect the last section to the first to close the circle.
  • Step 4: Step in, position at your waist, start spinning.
  • Too large? Remove 1–2 sections. Too tight? Add more.
