CozyLounge Set — Size Guide

The CozyLounge Set uses plus size XL labeling. The wide-leg pants have a relaxed, generous fit by design — if between sizes, stay at your normal size for the intended relaxed silhouette, or size up for maximum ease and drape.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • The tank is stretchy — when between sizes, your usual size works well.
  • The pants are relaxed cut — size up if you prefer extra drape.
