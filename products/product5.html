WinterBoost Boots — Size Guide

The WinterBoost Boots follow EU sizing and are designed for all foot widths. Measure your foot length from heel to toe. When between sizes, go up for comfortable, all-day wear — especially important for ankle boots.

How to Measure Your Foot

  • Step 1: Place your foot flat on a piece of paper.
  • Step 2: Mark the tip of your longest toe and the back of your heel.
  • Step 3: Measure the distance in cm. When between sizes, choose the larger size.
