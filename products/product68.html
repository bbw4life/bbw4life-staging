Is ice therapy safe to use every day on the face?
›

Yes — daily ice therapy is safe and beneficial for most skin types when applied correctly. The key is to keep the grid moving gently — never hold it still on one spot for more than 5 to 8 seconds. For sensitive skin, wrap the frozen grid in a thin cloth for your first few sessions to allow your skin to adapt. Most queens see visible improvement in puffiness and pore appearance within the first week of daily use.

Can I use the IceGlow Grid around my eyes?
›

Yes — the eye area is one of the most effective zones for ice therapy. Gently glide the frozen grid under your eyes and across your brow bone to reduce morning eye puffiness and dark circle visibility. The cold constricts blood vessels that cause under-eye swelling. Always glide outward from the inner corner — never press directly on the eyelid itself.
