How to Use BatanaBoost for Maximum Growth
1
Apply After Shampooing — Start at the Scalp Squeeze out excess water. Apply generously at the scalp, massaging in circular motions for 2 minutes to stimulate follicles and activate nutrient absorption.
2
Distribute Through Lengths — Focus on Damaged Areas Work from scalp to ends. Apply extra to brittle sections. Use a wide-tooth comb for even distribution without damage.
3
Leave On 5 to 30 Minutes 5–7 min for maintenance. 20–30 min under a shower cap for deep repair and maximum growth. Queens with thinning hair: use the long-leave method every wash day.

Rinse with Cool Water Cool water seals the cuticle, locking in moisture. Avoid hot water — it undoes everything the conditioner just delivered.
