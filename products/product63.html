How to Use BatanaBoost Conditioner for Maximum Growth
1
Apply After Shampooing — Start at the Scalp After shampooing, squeeze out excess water from your hair. Apply a generous amount of BatanaBoost Conditioner starting at the scalp, massaging gently with your fingertips in circular motions for 2 minutes. 2
Distribute Through Lengths — Focus on Damaged Areas Work the conditioner from scalp to ends, distributing evenly through all of your hair. For sections that are particularly dry, brittle or prone to breakage, apply an extra layer and gently press it into the hair shaft. Use a wide-tooth comb to ensure even distribution without causing additional damage to vulnerable strands. Every section deserves nourishment — do not rush this step.
3
Leave On for 5 to 30 Minutes — Longer for Deeper Results For regular maintenance, 5 to 7 minutes is sufficient. For intensive repair or maximum growth results, cover your hair with a shower cap and leave the conditioner on for 20 to 30 minutes. The warmth trapped under the cap opens the cuticle and allows the batana's active compounds to penetrate significantly deeper into the hair shaft and follicle. Queens dealing with severe damage or thinning should use the long-leave method every wash day.
4
Rinse with Cool Water — Seal the Results In Rinse thoroughly with cool water. Cool water closes the hair cuticle, sealing in the moisture and nutrients delivered by the conditioner so they remain active in your hair between washes. Avoid hot water during the rinse — it reopens the cuticle and allows everything you just delivered to escape. This single step makes the difference between results that last and results that disappear by day two.
