BusinessDress Shoes — Men's Size Guide

The BusinessDress Shoes are designed with a classic formal last that accommodates wider feet common in plus size men. Measure your foot length standing and refer to the chart. When between sizes, choose up for a more comfortable formal fit.

How to Measure

  • Foot Length: Stand on a flat surface and measure from heel to longest toe.
  • Foot Width: Measure the widest part of your foot across the ball.
  • Measure at the end of the day — feet are slightly larger after standing.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.

How do I care for these shoes to keep them looking sharp long-term?
›

Wipe after every wear with a slightly damp cloth to remove dust and surface dirt. Apply a matching-color shoe cream or polish every 2–3 weeks to maintain the shine and protect the upper. Store with shoe trees inside to hold the shape between uses — this is the single most effective way to extend the life of any formal shoe. Avoid prolonged exposure to rain or puddles. Treated well, these shoes will serve a king for years.
