DrawstringPants — Men's Size Guide

The DrawstringPants are designed for plus size men with a relaxed, comfortable fit. Measure your waist and hips for the best result. The drawstring waist adds flexibility — if you are between sizes, go up for maximum freedom of movement.

How to Measure

  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, relaxed.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • Inseam: Measure from your crotch to the ankle bone.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.
