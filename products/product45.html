PatentLoafers — Men Plus Size Shoe Guide

The PatentLoafers run true to size. Measure your foot length in centimeters and match to the chart below. If you are between two sizes, go up one size for comfort.

How to Measure Your Foot

  • Step 1: Place your foot on a flat piece of paper against a wall.
  • Step 2: Mark the longest point of your heel and longest toe.
  • Step 3: Measure the distance in centimeters.
  • Step 4: Match to the size chart below. When between sizes, go up.

Do these run large, small, or true to size?
›

The PatentLoafers run true to size. If you are between two sizes, go up. A slip-on with a little room is always more comfortable than one that is tight.

Are these comfortable enough to wear all day at a wedding?
›

Yes. The slip-on design removes pressure points. For wide feet, size up one and wear thin formal socks. Many kings wear them from ceremony to reception without discomfort.

How do I care for patent leather shoes?
›

Wipe clean with a soft damp cloth after each wear. For smudges, a little petroleum jelly restores the gloss. Store with shoe trees to hold the point-toe shape. Avoid water immersion.

I have never worn a loafer before — is it easy to walk in?
›

Loafers are one of the easiest formal shoes to walk in. Slip on, hold naturally, walk. No lacing, no adjustments. First-time loafer wearers consistently say they wished they had tried them sooner.
