PatentLoafers — Men Plus Size Shoe Guide

The PatentLoafers run true to size. Measure your foot length in centimeters and match to the chart below. If you are between two sizes, go up one size for comfort — a loafer with breathing room is always more comfortable than one that pinches. These are slip-on shoes, so a relaxed fit is perfectly intentional.

How to Measure Your Foot

  • Step 1: Place your foot on a flat piece of paper against a wall.
  • Step 2: Mark the longest point of your heel and longest toe.
  • Step 3: Measure the distance in centimeters.
  • Step 4: Match to the size chart below. When between sizes, go up.

Are these comfortable enough to wear all day at a wedding?
›

Yes. The slip-on design removes the pressure points that laced shoes create around the instep. The loafer cut gives your foot room to settle naturally during long events. For maximum comfort on a full wedding day, we recommend sizing up one if your foot is on the wider side, and wearing thin formal socks that allow your foot to breathe. Many kings report wearing them from ceremony to reception and through the evening without discomfort.

How do I care for patent leather shoes?
›

Patent leather is low-maintenance compared to regular leather. Wipe clean with a soft damp cloth after each wear. For fingerprints or smudges, a small amount of petroleum jelly on a soft cloth restores the gloss instantly. Avoid water immersion and extended rain exposure. Store with shoe trees or stuff with tissue paper to maintain the point-toe shape. Stored correctly, these shoes look sharp for years.

I have never worn a loafer before — is it easy to walk in?
›

Loafers are one of the easiest formal shoes to walk in. No lacing, no buckles, no adjustments mid-event. You slip them on, they hold naturally, and you walk. The slip-on fit for plus size men is especially comfortable because the wider foot sits securely inside without the pressure of a lace pulling down. First-time loafer wearers consistently say they wished they had tried them sooner.
