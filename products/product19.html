The LaceThong Set uses our plus size lingerie sizing. Measure your waist and hips. When between sizes, choose the larger size for the most comfortable and flattering fit — lace should drape beautifully, never pull.

How to Measure

  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, at the narrowest point.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
