The mask actively rebuilds this protein structure and restores moisture that the chemical process strips away. Queens with relaxed hair report dramatically less breakage and significantly improved manageability after consistent use.

How quickly will I see results?
›

Most queens notice a difference from the very first use — hair feels softer, smoother and more manageable immediately after rinsing. Frizz reduction is typically visible from application one. For structural repair of breakage and damage, consistent weekly use for two to four weeks produces the most significant cumulative results. Queens dealing with severe damage report transformative results within three to six weeks of twice-weekly use.

Can I use it as a leave-in treatment?
›

The DeepRepair Mask is formulated as a rinse-out treatment, not a leave-in. Leaving it in without rinsing may cause build-up that weighs hair down over time. For best results, follow the recommended application: apply to damp hair, leave for 15 to 45 minutes depending on damage level, then rinse thoroughly with cool water. The benefits are locked into the hair strand after rinsing — not dependent on remaining in the hair.

Is this suitable for natural, afro-textured hair?
›

Absolutely. The DeepRepair Mask is particularly effective for natural, afro-textured and coily hair types which are naturally more prone to dryness due to the coil pattern making it harder for natural oils to travel down the hair shaft. The intensive moisturizing formula is exactly what these hair types need — deep penetrating hydration that restores suppleness, defines curl pattern and significantly reduces frizz. Queens with natural hair report some of the most dramatic results with this mask.
