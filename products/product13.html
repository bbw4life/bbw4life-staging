MeshDuo Set — Size Guide

The MeshDuo Set is designed with a relaxed yet structured fit that flatters curvy silhouettes beautifully. The mesh layer has natural stretch for comfortable movement. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for the most elegant, flowing drape.

How to Measure Your Body

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size for maximum comfort and the most flattering drape of the mesh.

How do I care for this set?
›

Washing: Hand wash in cold water with gentle detergent. Machine wash on delicate cycle in a mesh laundry bag if needed. Never wring or twist the mesh fabric.

Drying: Lay flat to dry or hang gently away from direct sunlight. Avoid the dryer — heat damages mesh fabric.

Ironing: Use very low heat with a pressing cloth between the iron and fabric. Iron inside out. Queens who care properly for their MeshDuo Set report wearing it 20+ times with no damage to the mesh or shape loss.
