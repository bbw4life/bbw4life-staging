LoungeRobe — Size Guide

The LoungeRobe is designed with a generous, loose fit for maximum comfort. The fabric is soft and breathable. Measure your bust and use the chart below. When between sizes, go up for the most comfortable, relaxed drape — this robe is made to flow freely.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, the narrowest part of your torso.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below the waist.
  • Tip: The LoungeRobe has a relaxed loose fit — sizing up gives you the most comfortable, flowing drape.
