How do I wash this to keep it looking new longer?
›

Cold wash on a gentle cycle, then lay flat to dry. Never tumble dry — heat causes knit sweaters to shrink and lose their shape. Avoid ironing directly on the fabric — if you need to remove wrinkles, use a garment steamer or iron with a cloth barrier. Store folded, not hung — hanging a knit sweater stretches the shoulders over time. Treated properly, this sweater will serve a king for multiple seasons.
