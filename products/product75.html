JacquardPower Suit — Plus Size Size Guide

Two-piece set — blazer and wide-leg trousers sold together. The wrap blazer closes with a self-tie sash. Measure both bust and waist carefully. If between sizes, choose the larger — the sash adjusts the waist perfectly.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure your natural waist — where the self-tie sash will sit.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips for the trousers.
  • Between sizes? Always choose the larger size — the sash adjusts the blazer waist perfectly.

+

Bold prints on plus size bodies do not make the woman look larger. They make her look more powerful. The JacquardPower Suit was designed with this understanding from day one. The wrap blazer creates structure through the self-tie sash — so the bold brocade has a defined silhouette to inhabit. The wide-leg trousers create a sweeping vertical line that elongates. What shoes and accessories work best with a multicolor jewel-tone brocade?
+

The brocade is the statement — accessories should frame it, not compete. Nude, champagne or black pointed-toe heels let the suit do its work while extending the leg line. For jewellery, pick one colour from the print and match it — gold or rose gold earrings work with virtually every jewel-tone combination. Keep it minimal. One pair of earrings and a single delicate chain is all this suit needs. The print, the sash, the silhouette — they carry the room.
