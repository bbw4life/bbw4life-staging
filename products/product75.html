JacquardPower Suit — Plus Size Size Guide

This is a two-piece suit — blazer and wide-leg trousers sold together. The wrap blazer closes with a self-tie sash at the waist — measure both bust and waist carefully. The trousers are high-waisted wide-leg. If between sizes, choose the larger for the best drape and freedom of movement in both pieces.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest for the blazer fit.
  • Waist: Measure your natural waist — where the self-tie sash will sit.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips for the trousers.
  • Between sizes? Always choose the larger size — the sash adjusts the blazer waist perfectly.

What shoes and accessories work best with a multicolor jewel-tone brocade?
+

The brocade is already the statement — your accessories should frame it, not compete with it. Neutral shoes are the strongest choice: nude, ivory, champagne or black pointed-toe heels let the suit do its work while extending the leg line beautifully through the wide-leg trousers.
