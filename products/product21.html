CurveBikini — Size Guide

The CurveBikini is designed with a flattering plus-size fit. The high-waist bottom provides full coverage and excellent support. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for maximum comfort and the most flattering silhouette across all curvy body types.

How to Measure Your Body

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size for maximum comfort and the most flattering high-waist silhouette.

How do I care for this bikini to keep it beautiful?
›

After each wear: Rinse immediately in cool fresh water to remove chlorine or salt — this is the single most important step for preserving black fabric.

Washing: Hand wash with mild detergent in cool water. Gently squeeze — never wring or twist the fabric.

Drying: Lay flat in the shade to dry. Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods as UV exposure accelerates color fading in dark fabrics. Queens who follow this routine report their CurveBikini maintaining full color depth and elasticity for 2+ seasons.
