Will batana oil make my hair greasy?
›

When used in the correct amount, BatanaGlow Oil does not make hair greasy. Because batana penetrates rather than coats, it absorbs into the hair shaft rather than sitting on top. If you experience any heaviness, simply reduce the amount — the penetrating action means less oil delivers better results than more.
