TealEmpire Gown — Plus Size Size Guide

The TealEmpire Gown features a wrap-style V-neckline with a ruched waist knot — the knot sits at the natural waist and creates the waist definition. Bust and waist measurements are both important here. If between sizes, choose the larger size for the best drape and freedom of movement through the skirt.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline — where the ruched knot will sit.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • Between sizes? Choose the larger — the flowing skirt drapes better with room.

What shoes and accessories work best with deep teal?

Gold accessories are the most powerful pairing with deep teal — warm gold earrings and strappy gold heels create a combination that photographs as genuinely luxurious.
