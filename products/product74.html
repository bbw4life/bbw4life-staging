TealEmpire Gown — Plus Size Size Guide

The TealEmpire Gown features a wrap-style V-neckline with a ruched waist knot. Bust and waist measurements are both important. If between sizes, choose the larger for the best drape and freedom of movement.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline — where the ruched knot will sit.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • Between sizes? Choose the larger — the flowing skirt drapes better with room.

Does a sleeveless gown work for plus size women — and how does the ruched knot help?
+

The TealEmpire was designed so that the eye follows the V-neckline and the ruched knot — two deliberate visual points that create structure and shape. The ruched waist knot defines the silhouette even without a pronounced natural waist. Together, the V-neck and knot form a visual line that is both powerful and flattering. The dramatic flowing skirt then cascades to the floor — celebrating the body beneath it. This gown was not adapted for plus size queens. It was designed for them from the beginning.

Does deep teal work on darker skin tones?
+

Deep teal is one of the most striking colours on dark skin. The jewel-toned depth creates a combination that is powerful, vibrant and warm — without competing with the wearer. It photographs with extraordinary richness. Queens from West Africa, the Caribbean, Latin America and across the diaspora consistently describe it as the most striking colour they have worn at a formal event. On lighter skin tones, teal reads as cool and sophisticated. The colour works for every queen — that is why it was chosen.
