LooseJeans — Men Plus Size Size Guide

The LooseJeans are cut with a relaxed fit through the seat, hip and thigh — the silhouette is intentionally comfortable. The waistband sits at your natural waist. If you are between sizes, choose your usual size. If you prefer extra room in the seat and thigh, size up one.

How to Measure

  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, relaxed — not sucked in.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your seat.
  • Inseam: Measure from your crotch to your ankle.
  • Between sizes? Your usual size works — the relaxed cut gives natural room.
