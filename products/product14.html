MeshGlam Dress — Size Guide

The MeshGlam Dress is designed with a relaxed yet sculpting fit. The mesh stitching naturally follows your curves without pulling. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for the most flowing, elegant drape. The maxi length is consistent across all sizes.

How to Measure Your Body

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size for maximum comfort and the most flattering silhouette.

What makes mesh stitching different from a regular dress?
›

A regular dress drapes over your body. A mesh-stitched dress responds to it. The stitching lines create natural contouring that follows your silhouette rather than fighting it. On a curvy body, this creates a figure-flattering effect that plain fabric simply cannot achieve. The result is a dress that looks custom-made for your body — not just in your size, but in your shape.

How do I care for this dress?
›

Washing: Hand wash in cold water with gentle detergent. Machine wash on delicate cycle inside out if needed — this protects the mesh stitching.

Drying: Lay flat to dry or hang gently. Avoid the dryer — heat can affect the mesh texture.

Ironing: Use low heat with a pressing cloth. Iron inside out. Queens who care properly for their MeshGlam Dress report wearing it 15+ times with no shape loss or stitching damage. It is a dress built for real women who live their lives fully.
