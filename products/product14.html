MeshGlam Dress — Size Guide

The MeshGlam Dress is designed with a relaxed yet sculpting fit. The mesh stitching naturally follows your curves without pulling. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for the most flowing, elegant drape. The maxi length is consistent across all sizes.

How to Measure Your Body

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size for maximum comfort and the most flattering silhouette.

What makes mesh stitching different from a regular dress?
›

A regular dress drapes over your body. A mesh-stitched dress responds to it. The stitching lines create natural contouring that follows your silhouette rather than fighting it. The result is a dress that looks custom-made for your body — not just in your size, but in your shape.

Which color should I choose?
›

Khaki — warm, grounded, perfect for work and daytime elegance. Black — powerful and magnetic, ideal for evenings and photoshoots. White — radiant and striking, unforgettable at celebrations. Most versatile: Black. Most striking: White. Most wearable every day: Khaki.

Can I wear this for work or is it only for evenings?
›

The MeshGlam Dress works beautifully for professional settings. In Khaki or Black with closed-toe heels, it reads as sophisticated workwear. The mesh stitching adds texture that elevates you above a basic dress — making you look intentional and polished in any boardroom or client meeting.

How does the sizing run?
›

The MeshGlam Dress runs true to size. If you are between sizes, size up for a more elegant drape. Available from XL to 5XL. Check the size chart above for exact measurements.

What shoes pair best with the MeshGlam Dress?
›

Khaki: nude or tan block heels for day, gold strappy heels for evening. Black: stilettos, platform or metallic heels. White: nude or gold heels. For a bold street-style look, white sneakers with Black or Khaki work beautifully.

How do I care for this dress?
›

Washing: Hand wash cold with gentle detergent, or machine wash delicate inside out. Drying: Lay flat or hang gently — avoid the dryer. Ironing: Low heat with a pressing cloth, inside out. Treated with care, this dress holds its shape wear after wear.
