GeoPolo Shirt -- Men Plus Size Fit Guide

The GeoPolo has a regular fit -- not slim, not oversized. For a relaxed look, go true to size. For more room, size up one. Measure your chest and match to the chart.

How to Measure

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part, under the arms, tape level.
  • Shoulders: Measure from shoulder point to shoulder point across your back.
  • Between sizes? Go one size up.
