GeoPolo Shirt — Men Plus Size Fit Guide

The GeoPolo Shirt has a regular fit — not slim, not oversized. It sits naturally on the chest and shoulders without pulling or hanging. For a relaxed casual look, go true to size. For a looser summer feel, size up one. Measure your chest and match to the chart.

How to Measure

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, under the arms, keeping the tape level.
  • Shoulders: Measure from shoulder point to shoulder point across your back.
  • For a relaxed fit, go true to size. For more room, go one size up.
