BritishLoafers — Men's Formal Shoe Size Guide

The BritishLoafers fit true to size. If you are between two sizes, we recommend choosing the larger size for maximum comfort throughout the day. The slip-on design accommodates a slightly wider foot without losing the clean silhouette.

How to Measure Your Foot

  • Length: Stand on a flat surface and measure from your heel to the tip of your longest toe.
  • Width: Measure the widest part of your foot across the ball.
  • Time of day: Measure in the evening — feet naturally swell slightly during the day.
  • Between sizes? Choose the larger size for dress shoes — comfort is king.

What trousers pair best with the BritishLoafers?

Do these loafers fit wider feet?
