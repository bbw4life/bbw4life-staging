CropSlimPants — Men Plus Size Size Guide

The CropSlimPants are designed with a slim, tapered leg and a cropped ankle length. Measure your waist and hips carefully. The drawstring waist gives flexibility — if you are between two sizes, go up for comfort without losing the slim silhouette.

How to Measure

  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, relaxed.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • Inseam: Measure from your crotch to mid-ankle (cropped length).
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.

›

Black pairs with everything. Navy is slightly more elevated. Dark Gray is urban and flexible. Want to make a statement? Rust with a white tee is extraordinary. Start with Black or Navy.

Yes. The crop at mid-ankle is an intentional style cut — not a length error. For taller men it shows more of the shoe, which is the intended look. Pair with a clean sneaker or loafer to maximize the effect.
