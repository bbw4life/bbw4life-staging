How do I care for the LaceRomper?
›

Washing: Hand wash in cool water with a gentle detergent specifically designed for delicates or lace. Never use bleach. If using a machine, use a delicates bag on the gentlest cold cycle available.

Drying: Lay flat to dry naturally, away from direct sunlight. Never tumble dry — heat damages lace fibers and distorts the shape. Never wring or twist the lace.

Storage: Fold gently and store flat or rolled in a soft pouch. Avoid hanging — lace stretches under its own weight. Queens who care for their LaceRomper properly find the lace maintains its structure, softness and beauty through many months of wear.
