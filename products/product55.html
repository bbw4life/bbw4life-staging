Which shade should I choose — Dark Grey or Gold?
›

Dark Grey is the most versatile shade — it works beautifully on all skin tones and creates a defined, classic brow look suitable for everyday and special occasions. If you are buying your first BrowDye Pencil and are unsure, start with Dark Grey.

Gold is a warm, rich tone that complements medium to deep skin tones particularly well — it creates a softer, more sun-kissed brow effect that looks naturally luminous rather than sharply defined. If you prefer a warmer, more natural look or want brows that complement gold and warm-tone makeup, Gold is your shade. Many queens order both to match different looks and moods.

How do I remove the BrowDye Pencil at the end of the day?
›

The waterproof formula requires an oil-based or micellar makeup remover for clean, complete removal. Apply to a cotton pad, press gently over the brow area for 15 to 20 seconds to loosen the formula, then wipe away with a gentle stroke. Avoid rubbing aggressively — the formula releases cleanly with the right remover and gentle patience. Do not use water alone as the primary removal method as it will not fully dissolve the waterproof formula.

Can I use this pencil if my brows are very sparse?
›

Yes — in fact, the BrowDye Pencil was designed precisely for this. The micro-tip precision point allows you to draw individual hair strokes directly onto the skin in areas where brow hair is absent or very sparse. With light, layered strokes in the natural direction of growth, you can create the appearance of full, natural brows even from very sparse starting points. Pair with the BrowKit Pro for stencils that help define the shape before filling — perfect for sparse brows.
