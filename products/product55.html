Which shade should I choose — Dark Grey or Gold?

How do I remove the BrowDye Pencil?
›

Use an oil-based or micellar makeup remover. Press a cotton pad gently for 15–20 seconds, then wipe. The formula releases cleanly — do not use water alone.

Can I use this pencil if my brows are very sparse?
›

Yes — micro-tip draws individual hair strokes onto skin in sparse areas. Light, layered strokes in the direction of growth. Pair with BrowKit Pro stencils for perfect shape.
