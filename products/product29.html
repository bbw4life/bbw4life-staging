ContrastOne Piece — Size Guide

The ContrastOne Piece uses our plus size swimwear sizing. Measure your bust, waist, and hips. When between sizes, choose the larger size for the most comfortable and flattering fit — swimwear should celebrate your curves, never restrict them.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, at the narrowest point.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • Tip: When between sizes, always go up — swimwear should feel secure and flattering, not tight.
