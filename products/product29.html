ContrastOne Piece — Size Guide

Measure your bust, waist, and hips. When between sizes, choose the larger size for the most comfortable and flattering fit.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Around the fullest part of your chest, tape parallel to the ground.
  • Waist: Around your natural waistline, narrowest point.
  • Hips: Around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • Tip: When between sizes, always go up.
