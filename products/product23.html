How to Find Your Perfect Bra Size
1
Measure Your Band (Tour de dos) Wrap the tape firmly around your ribcage, just under your bust. Keep it parallel to the floor. Round to the nearest even number — this is your band size.
2
Measure Your Bust (Tour de poitrine) Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel and relaxed — not tight. Note this number.
3
Calculate Your Cup Size Subtract your band measurement from your bust measurement. Each 2.5cm difference = one cup size. 10cm = D, 12.5cm = E, 15cm = F, and so on.
4
If Between Sizes Always size up for maximum comfort. A slightly larger cup with a snug band gives the best lift and support for full figures.
Queen tip: Measure at the end of the day when your body is at its natural fullest — this gives the most accurate fit. Our Berline style assistant in the chat can also help you find your size if you are unsure.

How do I care for this bra?
›

Washing: Hand wash in cool water with gentle detergent. If using a machine, always use a mesh laundry bag on the delicate cycle with cold water. Never wring or twist the cups — this distorts the shape.

Drying: Lay flat to dry naturally. Never tumble dry — the heat damages the mesh elasticity and cup structure over time.

Storage: Store with cups nested inside each other, not folded flat — this preserves the cup shape. Queens who care for their SupportBra properly find it maintains its shape, support and comfort for many months of regular wear.
