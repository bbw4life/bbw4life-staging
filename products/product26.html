TubeBikini — Size Guide

The TubeBikini uses plus size XL labeling starting from 0XL. Measure your bust and hips for the best fit. The tube top provides excellent bust coverage — if you are between sizes, go up for maximum comfort and the most flattering drape.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.
