How to Apply — Your Daily Glow Ritual
1
Cleanse Your Face Start with a clean, dry face. Remove all makeup and impurities before applying the essence so the active ingredients can penetrate deeply without barrier.
2
Apply 3 to 5 Drops Dispense 3 to 5 drops of the Propolis Glow Essence onto your fingertips. Less is more — this concentrated formula works with small amounts for maximum effect.
3
Press Gently Into Skin Use gentle pressing motions — not rubbing — to apply the essence across your face. Focus on areas with acne marks, uneven tone, or dryness. Press inward for deeper absorption.
4
Let It Absorb Fully Wait 60 seconds before applying your moisturizer or sunscreen on top. The propolis and nicotinamide need time to bond with your skin cells for full brightening action.
5
Use Morning and Evening For best results, apply twice daily — morning and evening. Consistent use is the key to visible brightening and acne mark reduction. Most women notice visible results within 2 to 3 weeks.
Pro tip: Apply on slightly damp skin after toning for even deeper absorption — your skin absorbs active ingredients up to 40% more effectively when it is hydrated.

Many of our customers with deep and rich skin tones report the most striking results precisely because the contrast between marks and natural skin tone becomes visible progress over weeks.
