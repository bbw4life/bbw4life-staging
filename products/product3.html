BohoFlip Sandals — Size Guide

Designed for all foot widths. Measure heel to toe. When between sizes, go up for a comfortable, relaxed fit. The open-toe design accommodates wider feet beautifully.

How to Measure Your Foot

  • Step 1: Place your foot flat on a piece of paper.
  • Step 2: Mark the tip of your longest toe and the back of your heel.
  • Step 3: Measure the distance between the two marks in cm.
  • For sandals/flip flops: choose the larger size when between sizes for open, comfortable wear.
