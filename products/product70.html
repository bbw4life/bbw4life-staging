Does ivory work on all skin tones?
›

Ivory cream is one of the most universally flattering neutral tones in formal fashion. Unlike pure white, which can wash out lighter complexions, ivory has a warm, golden undertone that complements fair, medium, and deep skin tones equally. It creates a luminous, refined contrast against deep skin and sits harmoniously with lighter tones. Queens from Dakar to Paris to Port-au-Prince to Atlanta can wear this suit with full confidence.

How do I care for the PowerSuit?
›

For best results, hand wash cold or gentle machine wash on a delicate cycle with similar light colors. Do not bleach. For the blazer specifically, lay flat to dry to maintain the structured shape — do not tumble dry. A light steam iron on a low setting restores the crisp silhouette between wears. Store on a wide hanger to preserve the shoulder structure. Treated with care, this suit maintains its shape and color across many wears.
