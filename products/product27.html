RuffleOne — Size Guide

The RuffleOne uses plus size XL labeling starting from 0XL. Measure your bust and hips for the best fit. If you are between sizes, size up — a slightly relaxed fit drapes the ruffles more beautifully and gives you more freedom in the water.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.
