VintageSquare Dress — Size Guide

The VintageSquare Dress is designed with a relaxed but flattering fit that celebrates curves. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a comfortable, flowing silhouette. The printed fabric has natural stretch and movement.

How to Measure Your Body

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size for maximum comfort and confidence.

How do I care for this dress?
›

Washing: Hand wash in cold water with gentle detergent. If using a machine, use a delicate cycle in a mesh laundry bag. Avoid hot water — it can affect the printed fabric colors over time.

Drying: Lay flat to dry or hang gently. Avoid placing in direct sunlight for extended periods to preserve the richness of the print colors, especially the Red.

Ironing: If needed, use a low heat setting with a pressing cloth between the iron and the fabric. Iron inside out to protect the print. Queens who care for their VintageSquare properly report wearing it 30+ times with no fading or loss of shape.
