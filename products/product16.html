StripedMini Dress — Size Guide

The StripedMini Dress is cut with a flattering V-neck and body-skimming silhouette. The knit fabric has gentle stretch. Measure your bust and hips and use the chart below. When between sizes, go up for the most comfortable, flattering fit.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, the narrowest part of your torso.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below the waist.
  • When in doubt: Size up — the stretch fabric always drapes better with a little extra room.
