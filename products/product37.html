HaremPrints — Men Plus Size Size Guide

The HaremPrints are designed with a generous loose fit through the hip and thigh — the silhouette is intentionally relaxed. The waistband has an elastic or drawstring for full adjustability. If you are between two sizes, choose your usual size — the loose cut accommodates. If you prefer extra room in the seat, size up.

How to Measure

  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, relaxed — not pulled tight.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • Inseam: Measure from your crotch to mid-ankle (cuffed harem length).
  • When between sizes, your usual size is fine — the loose harem cut is forgiving.
