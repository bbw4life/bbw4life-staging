How long before I see real results with the NailRepair Lotion?
+

Most queens notice a difference in softness and texture within 5 to 7 days of daily use. Brightness and color improvement typically appear within 10 to 14 days. For severely damaged nails — particularly after heavy glue or acetone exposure — allow 3 to 4 weeks of consistent use for full restoration.

The key is regularity. The NailRepair Lotion works through daily accumulation, not a single application. Think of it the way you think about skincare — consistent daily care builds results that last.

Can I use this under press-on nails or after removing them?
+

Yes — and both timings are important. After removing press-on nails, the nail surface is often temporarily weakened. Applying the NailRepair Lotion immediately after removal helps restore moisture, reduce surface roughness and rebuild the nail plate before the next application.

Before applying press-ons, do not apply the lotion — it creates a barrier that reduces adhesion. Always apply the lotion during your rest periods between nail sets, not immediately before gluing new tips. This routine keeps your natural nails in optimal health throughout regular press-on use.

Is this safe for sensitive skin around the nails?
›

Yes. The NailRepair Lotion is formulated to be gentle on the skin surrounding the nail, including the cuticle and lateral nail folds. Apply only to the nail and cuticle area and avoid open cuts or wounds. If you have known sensitivities to cosmetic lotion ingredients, test a small amount on one nail first and wait 24 hours before full application. At BBW4LIFE, your beauty and your safety matter equally.

Can I apply nail polish or press-on nails after using this lotion?
›

For nail polish, wait 10 to 15 minutes after the lotion absorbs before applying. The lotion leaves a light moisture barrier that can slightly affect adhesion if the polish is applied too quickly. For press-on nails with glue, do not apply the lotion immediately before — use it during your rest periods between sets. The NailRepair Lotion is designed to work in the gaps between nail applications, keeping your natural nails strong and healthy for the long term.
