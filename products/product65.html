Why do knuckles get darker than surrounding skin?
+

Knuckle darkening is caused by melanin accumulation from repeated friction, pressure, and sun exposure. The joints flex constantly and produce more melanin as protection — creating a darker tone. KnuckleWhite Serum gently reduces this buildup and brightens the skin progressively.

How long before I see results?
+

Texture improves within 1–2 weeks. Visible brightening begins between weeks 3 and 5. Full results — significantly more even tone — appear after 6–8 weeks of twice-daily application.

Is this serum safe for all skin tones?
+

Yes — effective across all skin tones, including deeper melanin-rich complexions. No harsh bleaching agents. For sensitive skin, do a patch test on the inner wrist for 24 hours before use.
