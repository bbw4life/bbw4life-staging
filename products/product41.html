LeatherCasuals — Men's Size Guide

Measure your foot length standing up and refer to the chart below. If between sizes, go up for a more comfortable fit.

How to Measure

  • Foot Length: Stand on a flat surface. Measure from heel to longest toe.
  • Foot Width: Measure the widest part across the ball of your foot.
  • Measure at the end of the day — feet are slightly larger after walking.
  • When between sizes, choose the larger size for comfort.
