This sweatshirt is cut with a relaxed plus size fit — generous through the chest, shoulders, and body. Measure your chest and refer to the chart. For a very loose oversized feel, size up one. For the intended relaxed fit, go true to size.

How to Measure

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
  • Shoulders: Measure from shoulder point to shoulder point across the back.
  • When between sizes, choose the larger size for a more comfortable relaxed fit.
