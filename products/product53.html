How long do the BowNails actually stay on — and what makes them hold?
+

With a quality nail glue like the NailBond Glue, the BowNails hold for 5 to 10 days when properly applied to a clean, oil-free nail. With adhesive tabs, expect 1 to 3 days — ideal for events and special occasions.

The key factors for long wear are preparation and sizing. Clean nails thoroughly before application, size-match every tip individually, and apply the tip flush against the cuticle with no gaps. These three steps are what separate press-on nails that last from ones that lift by day two. At BBW4LIFE, beauty is about results — not just appearance.

Which design should I choose — Butterfly Gothic or Dark Red Bow?

The choice of a queen who doesn't need to be loud to be unforgettable. Both designs work on any skin tone. Many queens order both — one for daily boldness, one for special occasions.
