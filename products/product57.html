BrowKit Pro — Complete Usage Guide

The BrowKit Pro includes stencils in multiple arch shapes and a smooth waterproof color cream. The key is building color in light layers — this creates natural depth rather than a flat, drawn-on look. Always start lighter and build up.

What Is Included

ItemDescription
StencilsMultiple arch shapes — soft, defined and angular options
Color CreamLight Brown — smooth, buildable, waterproof formula
ApplicatorPrecision brush included for controlled application

Tips for Best Results

  • Start light: Always apply less cream than you think you need. Build up in thin layers for the most natural result.
  • Clean stencil between brows: Wipe your stencil with a damp cloth before switching to the other brow to avoid color transfer.
  • Blend edges: A clean spoolie or cotton swab along the outer brow edges softens any hard lines for a flawless finish.
  • Waterproof tip: For maximum longevity, avoid applying moisturizer or oily products on the brow area before use.
  • Storage: Keep the cream tightly sealed after use and store at room temperature away from direct sunlight.

How long do the results last on the skin?
›

The BrowKit Pro formula is waterproof and designed for all-day wear without touch-up. Under normal conditions — including heat, light perspiration and humidity — the result holds for 10 to 14 hours. For maximum longevity, ensure the brow area is clean and free from oily skin products before application. On very oily skin, a light mattifying primer on the brow bone can extend wear time.

Does the Light Brown shade work for very dark brows?
›

Yes. The Light Brown cream is highly buildable. For very dark natural brows, apply two full layers for a deeper, richer result that complements dark hair and deep skin tones beautifully. The warm undertone of the Light Brown shade looks natural against dark skin rather than appearing ashy or mismatched. Many queens with deep skin tones prefer it slightly built up to create a bold, striking brow look.

How do I remove the product at the end of the day?
›

The BrowKit Pro removes easily with standard makeup remover, micellar water or any oil-based cleanser. Apply to a cotton pad, hold gently against the brow for a few seconds to allow the product to lift, then wipe away cleanly. Do not rub aggressively — the formula releases with gentle pressure. After removal, cleanse the area normally as part of your evening skincare routine.
