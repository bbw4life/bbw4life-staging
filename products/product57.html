Tips for Best Results

  • Start light: Build up in thin layers for the most natural result.
  • Clean stencil between brows: Wipe with a damp cloth before switching sides.
  • Blend edges: A spoolie along the outer brow softens any hard lines.
  • Waterproof tip: Avoid oily products on the brow area before use.
  • Storage: Keep tightly sealed at room temperature away from sunlight.

›

Yes — Light Brown is highly buildable. Two full layers give a deeper result that complements dark hair and deep skin tones beautifully, with a warm undertone that looks natural, not ashy.

Can I use this if I have sparse or uneven brows?
›

Especially effective for sparse and uneven brows. The stencil covers the full shape, filling sparse areas completely. Queens with overplucked or thin brows consistently call this the most transformative beauty product they own.

How do I remove the product at the end of the day?
›

Removes easily with makeup remover, micellar water or any oil-based cleanser. Hold against the brow for a few seconds, then wipe gently — no need to rub.
