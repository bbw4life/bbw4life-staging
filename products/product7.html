NightChic Dress — Size Guide

The NightChic Dress uses plus size XL labeling. Measure your bust, waist and hips. If between sizes, go up for a relaxed, flattering fit — especially important with long-sleeve dresses.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.
