PremiumBikini — Size Guide

The PremiumBikini uses our premium plus size swimwear sizing. Measure your bust and hips carefully. When between sizes, choose the larger size for the most flattering and comfortable fit — this bikini is designed to celebrate your curves, not compress them.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, at the narrowest point.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • Tip: When between sizes, always size up — this ensures the best fit and maximum support.
