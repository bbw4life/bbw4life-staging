PremiumBikini — Size Guide

Measure your bust and hips carefully. When between sizes, choose the larger size for the most flattering fit.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Around the fullest part of your chest, tape parallel to the ground.
  • Waist: Around your natural waistline, narrowest point.
  • Hips: Around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • Tip: When between sizes, always size up.
