AirMesh Runners — Men's Size Guide

The AirMesh Runners are designed with a wider toe box and extra depth to accommodate the full plus size foot comfortably. Measure your foot length and refer to the chart below. If you are between sizes, go up for the best fit and comfort.

How to Measure

  • Foot Length: Stand on a flat surface and measure from heel to longest toe.
  • Foot Width: Measure the widest part of your foot across the ball.
  • Measure at the end of the day — feet are slightly larger after activity.
  • When between sizes, always choose the larger size.
