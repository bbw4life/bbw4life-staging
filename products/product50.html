Is this jacket warm enough for cold weather hiking or just for rain?
›

The HikeJacket is a waterproof wind-resistant outer shell — designed primarily to block wind and rain rather than to insulate by itself. For cold weather hiking, wear it as an outer layer over a fleece or thermal mid-layer. This is how serious outdoor gear works: the jacket stops the wind and rain while your inner layer traps body heat. In mild conditions, the jacket alone provides excellent protection and comfort.

How do I wash this jacket without damaging the waterproof coating?
›

Machine wash cold on a gentle cycle, then hang dry only. Do not use fabric softener — it degrades waterproof coatings. Do not tumble dry as heat damages both the outer fabric and the waterproofing treatment. After washing, the waterproof performance will be fully maintained when the jacket is cared for correctly. With proper care, this jacket will protect you through many adventures.
