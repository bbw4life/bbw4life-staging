RetroRun Sneakers — Size Guide

These sneakers are designed for all foot widths. Measure your foot length from heel to toe. When between sizes, go up for comfortable, all-day wear. The chunky sole gives you 3–4cm of extra height effortlessly.

How to Measure Your Foot

  • Step 1: Place your foot flat on a piece of paper.
  • Step 2: Mark the tip of your longest toe and the back of your heel.
  • Step 3: Measure the distance between the two marks in cm.
  • When between sizes, choose the larger size for max comfort.

How do I care for the laces and sole?
›

Laces: Remove the laces before cleaning. Hand-wash them with mild soap and warm water, then let them air-dry completely before re-lacing. Avoid machine washing — it weakens the fibers and makes them fray faster. Replace your laces every few months if you wear these regularly, for a fresh, clean look every time.

Chunky Sole: The thick rubber sole is durable but picks up dirt easily. Wipe it down with a soft damp cloth after each wear. For stubborn marks, use a gentle brush with mild soapy water and rinse clean. Never submerge the sneaker in water — it can damage the adhesive and weaken the sole bond over time. Let them air-dry away from direct heat and sunlight.

Upper: Wipe with a slightly damp cloth. For colored uppers like Green, Blue or Wine Red, avoid harsh chemicals that can fade the color. A little care goes a long way — queens who maintain their RetroRuns properly have worn them 30+ times and they still look brand new.
