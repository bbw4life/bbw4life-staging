How do I care for the StripedBikini?
›

After each use: Rinse thoroughly in cold fresh water immediately after swimming — this removes salt, chlorine, and sunscreen that degrade swimwear fabric over time.

Washing: Hand wash in cool water with a gentle detergent. Do not machine wash — the agitation breaks down stretch fibers. Do not bleach.

Drying: Lay flat to dry away from direct sunlight. Never tumble dry — heat damages the elastic and causes the print to fade. Queens who care for their StripedBikini find the color, print, and shape stay vibrant through a full season of use.
