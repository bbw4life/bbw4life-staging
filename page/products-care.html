From your lace lingerie to your stiletto heels, your mesh dress to your batana oil — every BBW4LIFE product deserves specific, loving attention.

Washing

  • Hand wash in cold water (30°C max)
  • Use a delicate mesh laundry bag if machine washing
  • Gentle detergent — no bleach ever
  • Wash lace pieces (LaceNight, LaceRomper) separately
  • Never tumble dry lace or mesh
  • No wringing — press gently in a towel
Turn your LeopardNight Set and LaceThong Set inside out before washing to protect the print and lace detailing.

Drying

  • Air dry flat on a clean towel
  • Keep away from direct sunlight — fades prints
  • Reshape the SupportBra cups while still damp
  • Hang LaceRomper on a padded hanger to dry
  • No tumble drying — destroys elasticity
  • Never hang a bra by its straps — distorts shape
Flat drying preserves the large-cup structure of your SupportBra and keeps lace panels intact.

Storage

  • Stack SupportBras cup-in-cup in a flat drawer
  • Fold LaceThong Set and LaceNight Dress flat
  • Store in a cool, breathable drawer
  • Keep away from direct perfume spray
  • Never fold underwire bras — breaks the wire
  • Avoid sealed plastic bags — traps humidity
Use lavender sachets near your LeopardNight Set to keep it fresh and fragrant between wears.

Longevity

  • Rotate between 3–4 lingerie pieces
  • Wash after every 2–3 wears
  • Check SupportBra elastic every 6–8 months
  • Avoid wearing mesh pieces (LaceThong Set) on rough surfaces
  • Don't wear lingerie in the pool or sea
  • No ironing on lace or mesh — ever
Proper care means your LaceRomper and SupportBra last up to 3× longer. That's real value for your curves.

Washing

  • Always check the label — especially for mesh & linen
  • Cold wash (30°C) for all printed dresses & tops
  • Wash LinenBreeze Dress separately — linen bleeds first wash
  • Machine wash MeshGlam, MeshDuo Set in a laundry bag
  • No hot water on stretch or jersey fabrics
  • No bleach on dark or printed pieces
Cold wash protects the paisley print on your PaisleyBelt Dress and prevents colour bleed on your PlaidOverall Dress.

Ironing & Steaming

  • Steam the LinenBreeze Dress — iron on low inside out
  • Use a pressing cloth on FloralFlounce printed areas
  • Steam the MeshDuo Set — never iron directly on mesh
  • Low heat on HikeJacket — polyester blend burns easily
  • No steam directly on the JacquardPower brocade fabric
  • Never iron sequins, pleating or ruched knot areas
A garment steamer is the best tool for your GlamSatin Dress and BlushLace Gown — it smooths fabric without flattening the drape.

Storage

  • Hang all dresses & blazers on padded wide hangers
  • Fold the ChristmasSweat & RoundNeck Sweatshirt — never hang knits
  • Hang the HikeJacket on a wide hanger to keep shoulders shaped
  • Store LooseJeans & DrawstringPants folded flat
  • No wire hangers — they distort plus-size shoulder seams
  • Avoid direct sunlight on printed fabrics in wardrobe
Gravity pulls the TurtleneckLux and StripedCollar Sweater out of shape — always fold them flat in a drawer.

Stain Removal

  • Act immediately — blot gently, never rub
  • Cold water only on the GlamSatin Dress — hot sets stains
  • White vinegar on deodorant marks on CollarShirt & GeoPolo
  • Diluted dish soap on grease stains on linen pieces
  • No hot water on fresh stains — ever
  • No bleach on DalmationShorts white fabric — yellows instantly
Baking soda + cold water paste works wonders on the LinenBreeze Dress and CozyLounge Set without damaging the fabric.

Skincare Products

  • Apply PoreClean Gel on clean, dry skin — avoid eye area
  • Use KnuckleWhite Serum on damp skin for better absorption
  • Apply Propolis Glow Essence before moisturiser, morning & night
  • Use MenGlow Cream as the last step of your morning routine
  • Apply IceGlow Grid Set after cooling in freezer for 10 min max
  • Do not mix Propolis Essence with retinol products
  • No PoreClean Gel on broken or irritated skin
Use your IceGlow Silicone Grid after applying the Propolis Glow Essence — the cold massage boosts absorption and reduces puffiness instantly.

Hair Care Products

  • Apply DeepRepair Hair Mask on damp hair — leave 10–15 min
  • Use BatanaGlow Oil on dry ends only — avoid the scalp if oily
  • Apply BatanaBoost Oil conditioner after shampooing, rinse after 3 min
  • Use both oils together for maximum repair on colour-damaged hair
  • Do not leave BatanaGlow Oil on hair overnight — stains pillows
  • No heat styling immediately after the DeepRepair Mask
Wrap your hair in a warm towel for 5 minutes after applying the DeepRepair Mask — the heat activates the formula and doubles the repair effect.

Makeup & Nail Products

  • Apply NailBond Glue to clean, oil-free nails only
  • Hold BowNails press-ons for 30 seconds after applying glue
  • Use NailRepair Lotion daily — massage into nail & cuticle
  • Apply BrowDye Pencil in short, feathery strokes for natural look
  • Use BrowKit Pro stencils before applying the cream for precision
  • Apply Curl & Volume Mascara from root to tip in zigzag motion
  • Do not share Mascara or BrowKit applicators
  • No NailBond Glue near eyes or mouth
Remove BowNails gently with warm water — never force them off. Use the NailRepair Lotion after removal to restore hydration to your natural nail.

Lip & Makeup Storage

  • Store Obsidian Lip Balm away from direct heat — melts above 35°C
  • Keep TearOff Lip Gloss upright and tightly closed after use
  • Store GingerClean Pads in original sealed pack — dry conditions only
  • Keep all skincare & makeup in a cool, dark drawer — not bathroom shelf
  • Clean IceGlow Silicone Grid with mild soap after each use
  • No NailBond Glue or BrowDye stored in direct sunlight — degrades formula
  • Never freeze Propolis Essence or KnuckleWhite Serum
A cool, dark makeup drawer extends the shelf life of all your BBW4LIFE beauty products by up to 30% — humidity in bathrooms degrades active ingredients fast.

Heels & Dress Shoes

  • Wipe Glam Heels & PowerHeels with a soft dry cloth after each wear
  • Apply leather conditioner on PatentLoafers & BritishLoafers monthly
  • Use shoe trees in BusinessDress Shoes to maintain shape between wears
  • Spray ColorStilettos & WinterBoost Boots with waterproof protector
  • Never stack heels — scratches the patent finish of PatentLoafers
  • No direct sunlight storage — fades colour on Glam Heels
Stuff your PowerHeels and WinterBoost Boots with tissue paper when storing — it holds the shape of the toe box and prevents creasing.

Sneakers & Flat Shoes

  • Clean AirMesh Runners & HollowSneakers with a soft damp brush
  • Remove insoles of TrendTrainers after sport — air dry separately
  • Wipe LeatherCasuals with a dry cloth — never soak leather
  • Clean BohoFlip Sandals straps with mild soapy water & air dry flat
  • No machine wash for any shoes in our collection
  • No direct heat drying — shrinks mesh & cracks soles
Sprinkle baking soda inside your AirMesh Runners and TrendTrainers overnight to absorb odours — shake out before wearing the next day.

Bikinis & Swimwear

  • Rinse all bikinis in cold fresh water immediately after swimming
  • Hand wash StripedBikini, TubeBikini & RuffleOne with mild soap
  • Lay BandageBikini & ContrastOne Piece flat to dry in the shade
  • Wash SolidSexy Bikini & CurveBikini after every sea or pool use
  • No machine wash for any bikini — elastic breaks instantly
  • No wringing — chlorine + wring = ruined lycra
Chlorine and sunscreen are the biggest enemies of your PremiumBikini. Rinse in cold water immediately after every use — it's the single most important care step.

Storage — Shoes & Swimwear

  • Store heels in individual dust bags or original boxes
  • Keep RetroRun Sneakers in a ventilated shoe rack — not a sealed box
  • Fold bikinis flat — never ball them up in a wet bag
  • Store swimwear completely dry before putting in a drawer
  • No stacking heavy shoes on top of heels — crushes toe box
  • Never store wet swimwear in a plastic bag — causes mould & odour
Place a silica gel sachet in your shoe storage area to absorb moisture — it keeps your LeatherCasuals and PatentLoafers in perfect condition year-round.

Label Guide

Decode Your
Care Labels

Those little symbols on your BBW4LIFE garment labels are your piece speaking to you. Here's exactly what they mean.

Wash tub Machine washable. Number inside = max temp in °C (most BBW4LIFE pieces: 30°C)
Tub + line Machine wash on gentle / delicate cycle only — applies to all mesh & lace pieces
Circle crossed Do NOT dry clean — common on our stretch-blend dresses and swimwear
X in square Do NOT tumble dry — mandatory for all lingerie, bikinis & lace pieces
Iron symbol Dots = heat level. 1 dot = low (polyester). 2 dots = medium (linen). 3 dots = high cotton only
Hand in tub Hand wash only — required for LaceNight Dress, LaceRomper, LaceThong Set & all lingerie
Circle Dry clean only — may appear on JacquardPower Suit & BlushLace Gown formal pieces
Triangle Bleaching allowed if empty. Crossed triangle = absolutely no bleach — applies to all printed & dark pieces

Care FAQ

We strongly recommend hand washing all lingerie — LaceNight Dress, LaceRomper, LaceThong Set, and LeopardNight Set — in cold water (30°C max). If you must use a machine, always place the piece in a delicate mesh laundry bag on the gentlest cycle. Never use the spin cycle or tumble dry for any of these pieces.

Hand wash your SupportBra in cold water with a gentle detergent. Never wring it — press it gently in a clean towel. Reshape the cups immediately while damp and lay flat to dry. Store cup-in-cup in a flat drawer, never fold it, and never hang it by the straps. The elastic in large-cup bras degrades with heat — keep it away from the dryer and direct sunlight at all times.

Rinse your bikini (StripedBikini, CurveBikini, TubeBikini, BandageBikini, RuffleOne or PremiumBikini) immediately in cold fresh water after every swim. Chlorine and sea salt both degrade lycra very quickly if left to dry without rinsing. Then hand wash with a mild soap, press gently in a towel, and lay flat to dry in the shade. Never wring, never machine wash, never tumble dry.

For the GlamSatin Dress and MeshGlam Dress, always use a garment steamer — never iron directly on satin or mesh. For the LinenBreeze Dress, you can iron on low heat inside out with a pressing cloth between the iron and fabric. Never iron the ruched knot areas, lace panels, or any printed sections. When in doubt, steam — it is always safer than a direct iron on BBW4LIFE fabrics.

Apply the DeepRepair Hair Mask on damp, freshly shampooed hair. Distribute from mid-length to ends, then wrap your hair in a warm towel for 5–10 minutes before rinsing — the heat activates the formula. For the BatanaGlow Oil, apply a few drops to dry ends only after styling. Avoid the scalp if you have oily hair. You can use both in the same routine: mask first, rinse, then a drop of BatanaGlow Oil to seal and shine.

For heels (Glam Heels, PowerHeels, ColorStilettos, PatentLoafers, BritishLoafers), store in individual dust bags with tissue paper stuffed inside to hold the toe box shape. Never stack heels. For sneakers (AirMesh Runners, RetroRun, TrendTrainers, HollowSneakers), keep in a ventilated shoe rack — sealed boxes trap moisture and cause odour. Always remove insoles after sport use and let them air dry separately.
