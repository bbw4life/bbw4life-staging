Soft Queen

Fuller through the middle, with gorgeous shoulders and legs that command every room. Your silhouette radiates strength, softness and confidence. Empire cuts and wrap styles are your secret weapons — they celebrate every inch of you.

Style tip: Define your waist with wrap cuts and elongate beautifully with V-necklines.

Curve Goddess

Balanced curves above and below a defined waist — the silhouette that turns heads without trying. You were born to wear fitted, bodycon and wrap styles. Every curve is a gift, and yours deserve to be celebrated out loud.

Style tip: Show off that waist with belted dresses and form-fitting cuts that hug your goddess figure.

Bold Bottom

Wider hips and thighs with a beautifully slimmer upper body — a shape that is pure elegance personified. Your curves below are your power. Flared silhouettes, wide necklines and bold top details will have you feeling like the royalty you are.

Style tip: Draw eyes upward with wide necklines and bold prints on top — let those hips do the talking.
