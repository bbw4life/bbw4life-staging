1. Order Confirmation & Email

As soon as your payment is confirmed, BBW4LIFE sends you an order confirmation email with your order details and a summary of what to expect next.

  • You receive your confirmation within minutes of confirmed payment
  • Check your inbox (and spam/junk folder) for an email from support@bbw4life.com
  • Your order number is included in this email — save it for any future support requests
  • If you do not receive a confirmation email within 30 minutes, contact us immediately at support@bbw4life.com

No confirmation email? Check your spam folder first. If it is not there, email support@bbw4life.com with the email address you used at checkout and we will resolve it within 2 hours.

2. Physical Product Shipping

All products available in the BBW4LIFE Store — fashion, shoes, beauty, and accessories — are shipped by our trusted partner suppliers. BBW4LIFE does not manufacture or warehouse products directly.

Once you place an order for a physical product:

  • Your order is transmitted to the relevant partner supplier within 1 business day
  • The supplier prepares and dispatches your order within the processing time displayed on the product page
  • You receive a dispatch confirmation email with your tracking number once the order leaves the warehouse
  • Delivery is handled by the carrier selected at checkout (carrier options vary by destination)

Order processing days: Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays. Orders placed on weekends are processed the next business day.

4. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address, the weight of your order, and the shipping method selected. You will always see the exact shipping cost before confirming your payment — there are no hidden fees added after purchase.

Standard Shipping

The most economical option. Delivery times vary by destination. Cost displayed at checkout before payment.

Express Shipping

Faster delivery where available. Higher cost, significantly shorter transit times. Availability depends on destination.

Free Shipping

Orders over a certain threshold qualify for free standard shipping to eligible destinations. Threshold and eligible regions shown at checkout.

Order Confirmation

Sent instantly by email at no cost. Your order details, tracking, and updates arrive directly in your inbox.

5. Order Tracking

Once your physical order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking confirmation email containing your tracking number and a link to follow your package in real time.

  • Tracking updates may take 24–48 hours to appear after dispatch — this is normal and does not indicate a problem
  • Tracking is provided by the carrier handling your shipment (varies by destination and shipping method)
  • If your tracking number shows no updates for more than 7 business days, contact our support team

Tracking issue? Email support@bbw4life.com with your order number and tracking number. We will investigate with the carrier within 3 business days.

6. International Shipping

BBW4LIFE ships physical products to most countries worldwide. We want every woman, wherever she is, to have access to styles that celebrate her. International orders are subject to the following conditions:

  • Shipping availability and costs for your specific country are shown at checkout
  • International delivery times are estimates — customs processing in your country may add additional time
  • BBW4LIFE is not responsible for delays caused by customs authorities, local postal services, or import regulations in your country
  • Some products may not be available for shipping to all destinations due to partner supplier restrictions — you will be notified at checkout if this applies

If your country is not listed at checkout or you are unsure about shipping availability to your location, contact us before placing your order: support@bbw4life.com

7. Customs & Import Duties

For international orders, your package may be subject to customs inspection and import duties or taxes levied by your country's customs authority. These charges are not included in your BBW4LIFE order total and are the sole responsibility of the recipient.

Charge Type Who Pays? When?
Import duties & taxes Customer (recipient) Upon delivery or customs clearance
Customs inspection delays N/A — outside BBW4LIFE control Variable — depends on destination country
Return-to-sender due to customs refusal Customer responsible for return shipping If package is refused at customs

We recommend checking your country's import regulations before placing an international order. BBW4LIFE cannot be held responsible for customs charges, delays, or refusals.

8. Lost or Damaged Orders

While the vast majority of BBW4LIFE orders arrive safely and on time, we understand that issues occasionally occur. Here is exactly how we handle them:

Lost Package

  • If your tracking shows no movement for more than 10 business days beyond the estimated delivery date, contact us at support@bbw4life.com
  • We will open an investigation with the carrier on your behalf
  • If the package is confirmed lost, we will offer either a full refund or a replacement shipment — your choice
  • Resolution typically takes 7–14 business days from the date of investigation opening

Damaged Package

  • If your order arrives visibly damaged, photograph the packaging and the product immediately before opening or disposing of anything
  • Email us within 48 hours of delivery at support@bbw4life.com with your photos and order number
  • We will assess the damage and offer a replacement or refund within 5 business days

Important: Claims for lost or damaged items must be submitted within 30 days of the estimated delivery date. Claims submitted after this period may not be eligible for resolution.

Support email: support@bbw4life.com

9. Incorrect Delivery Address

Please double-check your delivery address carefully before confirming your order. BBW4LIFE and our partner suppliers process orders quickly — address changes after confirmation are not always possible.

  • If you realize you entered an incorrect address within 1 hour of placing your order, contact us immediately at support@bbw4life.com — we will do our best to correct it before dispatch
  • Once an order has been dispatched, we cannot change the delivery address
  • If a package is returned to sender due to an incorrect address provided by the customer, reshipping costs are the customer's responsibility
  • If a package is delivered to an incorrect address due to a customer error, BBW4LIFE cannot be held responsible for the loss

Address correction window: Contact us within 1 hour of order placement for the best chance of correction. After dispatch, no address changes are possible.

Support email: support@bbw4life.com
