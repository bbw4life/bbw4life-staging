3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for exactly these purposes — and nothing else:

  • Order processing & delivery: Confirm your purchase, coordinate fulfillment with our logistics partners, and keep you updated on your shipment
  • Support: Read and respond to your questions, technical issues, or return requests with care and accuracy
  • Order confirmation emails: Send transactional emails that are part of your purchase experience — these are not marketing, they are service communications
  • Newsletter: Share style inspiration, community stories, and exclusive member offers — only if you explicitly subscribed, always with a one-click unsubscribe link
  • Return & refund verification: In return or refund cases, review the information you voluntarily provide (proof of purchase, photos of item condition)
  • Site improvement: Analyze anonymized, aggregated usage data to improve the shopping experience and content quality
  • Fraud prevention: Use IP addresses and purchase patterns to detect and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Legal compliance: Retain purchase records for the legally required period (5 years in most jurisdictions)

5. Payment Processing

BBW4LIFE does not store, process, or have access to your credit card or bank account details. All payments are processed by Stripe and PayPal — two of the most trusted PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processors in the world. We also accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

When you enter payment information, it goes directly to the secure servers of Stripe or PayPal depending on your chosen method. BBW4LIFE receives only a transaction confirmation and the last 4 digits of your card for your records. Your full card details, PayPal credentials, and banking information never pass through or are stored on BBW4LIFE systems.

Stripe privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy  •  PayPal privacy policy: paypal.com/privacy

Refund requests: billing@bbw4life.com

Standard refund: Items must be returned in their original, unworn condition within the stated return window. Refunds are processed within up to 30 days (may vary depending on our logistics partners).

Dispute or damaged item: Please contact us with photos of the item and your order number. Our team reviews every case with care and responds within 5 business days.

Subscription cancellation: You may cancel at any time. Processing takes up to 10 days. A partial refund for unused time may be issued via your original payment method — or another method if requested at least 5 days before processing begins.

Include in all requests: Your purchase email and order number for faster processing.

8. Data Security

We take the following concrete measures to protect your data:

  • SSL encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/SSL. The padlock in your browser confirms this.
  • Secure hosting: Our servers are hosted with providers that maintain physical and digital security including firewalls, intrusion detection, and regular audits.
  • Access control: Only team members who need your data for their specific role can access it. A support agent does not have access to your billing history. A billing agent does not have access to your personal preferences.
  • Password hashing: Passwords are never stored in plain text. They are hashed using one-way encryption — BBW4LIFE cannot see your password.
  • Personal data isolation: Your style preferences and personal details are stored separately from your billing data and are never used for external marketing analysis.
  • Return request data: Photos or documentation submitted for return or refund requests are used solely for that evaluation and deleted once the request is resolved.

No system is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or any jurisdiction with equivalent data protection laws, you have the following rights over your personal data. These rights are real, actionable, and free to exercise:

Right of Access

Request a copy of all personal data BBW4LIFE holds about you. We provide it in a readable format within 30 days.

Right of Rectification

Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data we hold. We update it within 30 days.

Right to Erasure

Request complete deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"). We delete all non-legally-required data within 30 days.

Right to Portability

Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (CSV or JSON) so you can transfer it elsewhere.

Right to Object

Object to any specific use of your data — including marketing emails, analytics, or profiling. We honor your objection immediately.

Right to Restriction

Limit how we use your data without deleting it — for example, keep your account active but stop all marketing use.

How to Exercise Your Rights

Email support@bbw4life.com with your full name, the email address on your account, and a description of the right you wish to exercise. We confirm receipt within 48 hours and fulfill the request within 30 days.

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority (e.g. ICO in the UK, CNIL in France, or the equivalent in your country).
