1. Our Approach to Refunds

BBW4LIFE was founded on love, honesty, and the belief that every curvy woman deserves to feel beautiful and respected — including when something goes wrong with an order. We do not hide behind walls of legal text designed to discourage returns. If you have a legitimate concern, we want to hear it and resolve it fairly.

Because BBW4LIFE works in partnership with trusted product suppliers and logistics partners, some timelines and conditions depend on those partners. We are transparent about those dependencies in each section below.

For any refund or return request, contact our billing team directly at billing@bbw4life.com and include your purchase email and order number for faster processing.

2. Product Returns

BBW4LIFE's Shop offers a carefully curated selection of fashion, shoes, and beauty products sourced through trusted partner suppliers. Because products are not manufactured by BBW4LIFE directly, returns are processed in collaboration with our partners.

Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible for a product return and refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • The product must be returned in its original condition — unworn, unwashed, undamaged, and in its original packaging with tags attached
  • The return request must be submitted within a reasonable delay after reception (typically within 14 days — contact us if your situation is exceptional)
  • You must provide proof of purchase (order confirmation email or order number)

Return Timeline

Step Action Timeline
1 Submit your return request to billing@bbw4life.com As soon as possible after reception
2 Our team reviews your request and sends return instructions Within 5 business days
3 You return the item following the instructions provided Within the delay specified
4 Partner validates the return and triggers the refund Up to 30 days after validation

⚠️ Important: Before You Order

To avoid return complications, please take these steps before placing your order:

  • Check your size carefully — consult the size guide on the product page before ordering clothing or footwear. Sizing can vary between suppliers.
  • Verify your delivery address — ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate before confirming your order.

BBW4LIFE cannot be held responsible for delivery issues caused by an incorrect address provided at checkout.

3. Damaged or Incorrect Item

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or if you receive the wrong item, BBW4LIFE will always find a solution. We stand behind every product in our store.

Photograph Immediately

Before opening or disposing of any packaging, photograph both the packaging and the item clearly. This is essential for your claim.

Contact Within 7 Days

Email us within 7 days of delivery with your photos and order number. Early contact gives us the best options to help you quickly.

Team Review

Our team reviews your photos and order details carefully and responds within 5 business days with a proposed resolution.

Replacement or Refund

If your claim is validated, we will offer a replacement shipment or a full refund — your choice. No return shipping required for damaged items.

How to Submit a Damaged or Incorrect Item Claim

  1. Email billing@bbw4life.com with subject line: "Damaged / Incorrect Item"
  2. Include your full name, purchase email, and order number
  3. Attach clear photos of the item and packaging
  4. Briefly describe the issue — what you received and what was wrong
  5. Our team will confirm receipt within 48 hours and notify you of the outcome within 5 business days

Note: Claims for damaged or incorrect items must be submitted within 7 days of delivery. Photos submitted for this evaluation are used solely to assess your claim and deleted once the case is closed.

4. Order Cancellation

At BBW4LIFE, we process orders quickly to get your items to you as fast as possible. Here is exactly what happens if you need to cancel:

Step What Happens Timeline
1 You request cancellation via email to support@bbw4life.com Before order dispatch only
2 We verify whether the order has already been dispatched Within 1 business day
3 If not yet dispatched, cancellation is confirmed and processing begins Within 1–3 business days
4 Full refund issued to your original payment method Up to 30 days (payment provider dependent)

After Dispatch — No Cancellation Possible

Once your order has been dispatched, cancellation is no longer possible. However, you may initiate a return once the item is received, subject to the conditions in Section 2.

Changing Your Refund Payment Method

If you wish to receive your refund via a different payment method than the one used at purchase, you must inform us at least 5 days before the refund process begins. Contact billing@bbw4life.com with your request. We cannot modify the payment destination once processing has started.

5. How Refunds Are Processed

All refunds at BBW4LIFE follow the same clear process:

  • Method: Refunds are returned via the same payment method used at purchase (Stripe, PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, etc.) — unless you request an alternative at least 5 days before processing
  • Timeline: Up to 30 days from validation — this window accounts for processing time on our end and with our payment partners
  • Currency: Refunds are issued in the same currency as the original transaction
  • Notification: You will receive an email confirmation once your refund has been initiated
  • Bank delay: After we initiate the refund, your bank or payment provider may take an additional 3–10 business days to reflect the amount in your account — this is outside our control

Always include in your refund request: Your purchase email address and your order number. This allows our team to process your request without delays.

Refund email: billing@bbw4life.com

Response time: We confirm all refund requests within 48 hours and resolve them within 30 days.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

We are committed to fairness — for you and for every member of the BBW4LIFE family. The following situations are generally not eligible for a refund:

  • Products that have been worn, washed, damaged by the customer, or returned without original packaging and tags
  • Beauty and skincare products that have been opened or partially used (for hygiene reasons)
  • Refund requests submitted without required documentation (proof of purchase, photos for damage claims)
  • Requests made after the eligible return window has passed without prior contact
  • Situations where incorrect address information was provided at checkout and delivery failed
  • Purchases made under a promotional offer that explicitly stated no refunds at the time of purchase

If you are unsure whether your situation is eligible, please reach out before assuming the answer is no. We review every case individually and in good faith.

7. Tips to Avoid Issues

A smooth experience starts before you place your order. Here are our top recommendations:

  • Check sizing charts: Before ordering clothing or footwear, always consult the size guide on the product page. Sizing can vary between suppliers.
  • Double-check your delivery address: Verify your full address — street, number, city, country, and postal code — before confirming any order.
  • Keep your confirmation email: Your order number is in your confirmation email. Save it — you will need it for any support request.
  • Inspect your order on arrival: Open and check your items as soon as they arrive. Report any damage or discrepancy within 7 days.
  • Check beauty product ingredients: If you have known skin sensitivities, review the full ingredient list on the product page before ordering.
  • Contact us early: If something feels off — with delivery, product quality, or your order — reach out sooner rather than later. Early contact gives us more options to help you.
